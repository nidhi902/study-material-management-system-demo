About StudyShare

StudyShare is a simple web platform built for teachers and students. Teachers can upload study materials, notes, and assignments, while students can access and download them anytime, anywhere.

Our mission is to make learning more collaborative and easily accessible. Built with ❤️ using Django and Bootstrap.
